About Dalgona Coffee

Dalgona coffee is a South Korean coffee dish, which went viral during the Lockdown. The dish can be made from a few recipes which are ready available in your kitchen cupboard. What are you waiting for?

Ingredients

  • Instant coffee power or Expresso Granules
  • Hot Water
  • Sugar
  • Milk

Steps

Step 1: add coffee power and hot water
Add 2 tablespoons of coffee power and a cup of hot water. Add 2 teblespoons of sugar to the mixture
Step 2: Stir the mixture well
Use a handheld mixer and stir the mixture well, till it turns creamy and fluffy
Step 3: Serve it with Milk
Add a cup of hot milk and add the fluffy mixture to milk. serve it with a spoon and a straw. Now stir, stir and stir to enjoy the dish
